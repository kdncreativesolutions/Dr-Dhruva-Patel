About Dr. Dhruva Patel

Your trusted partner in periodontal care and oral health

Dr. Dhruva Patel, MDS

Master of Dental Surgery - Periodontology

Dr. Dhruva Patel is a distinguished periodontist with over 15 years of specialized experience in treating gum diseases and performing complex dental implant procedures. Her commitment to excellence and patient-centered care has made her one of the most trusted names in periodontal therapy.

With a Master's degree in Periodontology and extensive training in advanced surgical techniques, Dr. Patel combines traditional periodontal therapy with cutting-edge technology to deliver optimal treatment outcomes.

She is particularly renowned for her expertise in laser gum surgery, dental implantology, and comprehensive smile rehabilitation programs that have transformed thousands of lives.
