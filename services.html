Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our periodontal treatments

How do I know if I need periodontal treatment?

Signs include bleeding gums, persistent bad breath, gum recession, loose teeth, or pain when chewing. A comprehensive evaluation can determine if treatment is needed.

Are dental implants painful?

Modern implant procedures are performed under local anesthesia with minimal discomfort. Most patients report that the procedure is much easier than they expected.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last a lifetime. Success rates are over 95% at 10 years and continue to improve with advances in technology.

What makes laser gum surgery better?

Laser treatment offers less bleeding, reduced swelling, faster healing, minimal discomfort, and often eliminates the need for traditional surgical procedures.
