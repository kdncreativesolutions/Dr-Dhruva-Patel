Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about appointments and visits

How do I prepare for my first appointment?

Bring a list of current medications, previous dental X-rays (if available), insurance information, and a list of questions or concerns you'd like to discuss.

What should I expect during the consultation?

A comprehensive examination, discussion of your dental history, treatment planning, and detailed explanation of recommended procedures with cost estimates.

Do you provide emergency care?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency care for periodontal and implant-related urgent issues. Call our emergency line for immediate assistance.

How far in advance should I book appointments?

For regular check-ups, 2-4 weeks is typically sufficient. For complex procedures, we may need 4-6 weeks. Emergency appointments are accommodated same-day when possible.
